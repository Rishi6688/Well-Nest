1
Boil the water — Boil 1 cup of water in a saucepan on medium-high heat until rolling bubbles appear.
2
Add ginger — Peel and crush 1 inch of fresh ginger. Drop it into the boiling water.
3
Add pepper & simmer — Crush 3 black peppercorns and add to the pot. Reduce to low. Simmer 3 minutes.
4
Cool then add honey — Remove from heat. Wait 2 minutes. Stir in 2 tsp honey — never add to boiling liquid.
5
Squeeze lemon & serve — Squeeze half a lemon. Stir gently. Pour into a cup and sip slowly while warm.
